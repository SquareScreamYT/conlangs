### Word Order

Gaselian uses SVO word order:

```text
[subject] i [verb] e [object]
```

```text
mi  i    maza e   lino
1SG PRED eat  OBJ apple
"I eat an apple"
```

```text
du  i    guno
2SG PRED drink
"you drink"
```

```text
zi  e   ezi
3SG OBJ lizard
it is a lizard"
```

Adjectives and adverbs come before nouns and verbs:

```text
[subject] i [adverb] [verb] e [adjective] [object]
```

```text
[subject] a [adjective]
```

```text
si  i    rapi maja e   rohe rino
3SG PRED fast eat  OBJ red  apple
"they quickly ate the red apple"
```

```text
rino  a   rohe
apple ADJ red
"apples are red"
```

```text
rohe yace  rino
red  round apple
"red round apple"
```

Words change depending on where it is placed:

```text
je  i    maja
1SG PRED eat
"I eat"
```

```text
rino  e   maja
apple OBJ food
"apples are food"
```

```text
rino  a   maja
apple ADJ edible
"apples are edible"
```

Possesion is marked using "de":

```text
[possessed] de [possessor]
```

```text
liba de  papa   de  je
book GEN father GEN 1SG
"my father's book"
```

```text
rino  e   de  je
apple ADJ GEN 1SG
"the apple is mine"
```

# Clong

## Preface

Clong is a loglang. It is inspired by [Toaq](https://toaq.net/refgram/introduction/) and [Mini](https://minilanguage.com/). Similar to Toaq, it is designed to be a human language, while also preserving disambiguity.

## Background

## Phonology

Clong has 20 consonant phonemes and 5 vowel phonemes. Syllables have the form (C)V, as in most Austronesian languages.

### Consonants

### Phonotactics

(C)V where:

* C can be any consonant
* V can be any vowel

### Orthographic Conventions

* The first word of every sentence is capitalised.
* Proper nouns are capitalised.
* Subordinate clauses are surrounded by commas.
* Declarative sentences end with a full stop, interrogative ones end with a question mark, and imperatives and interjections end with an exclamation mark.

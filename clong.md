## Phonotactics

* **Syllable structure:** (C)V(C)
* **Onset:** Optional; any consonant except /ŋ/ and /ŋʲ/
* **Nucleus:** Compulsory; any vowel /i ɨ e a o u/
* **Coda:** Optional; any non-palatalised consonant. /j/ and /w/ are written as ⟨i⟩ and ⟨u⟩ in codas. [ʒ] is written ⟨rz⟩.

## Grammar

### Pronouns

|    | 1   | 2   | 3   | NDEF |
|----|-----|-----|-----|------|
| SG | vi  | ti  | si  | ki   |
| PL | vią | tią | nią | ki   |

> **Note:** Nouns do not decline for plurality.

### Word Order

Clong is SVO. Objects are marked with **e**, and pronouns stack onto the verb as a suffix.

```text
gatų szaną e pingą
cat  see.PRS OBJ apple
"the cat sees an apple"
```

```text
szanąvi e pingą
see.PRS.1SG OBJ apple
"I see an apple"
```

### Tense

| Tense   | Suffix |
|---------|--------|
| Past    | į      |
| Present | ą      |
| Future  | ę      |

### Negation

Negation is marked with **ny**:

```text
szanąviny e pingą
see.PRS.1SG.NEG OBJ apple
"I do not see an apple"
```

### Questions

Questions are marked with **li**:

```text
liszanąti e pingą?
Q.see.PRS.1SG OBJ apple
"Do you see an apple?"
```

### Imperative

Imperatives use **da**:

```text
daszaną e pingą!
IMP.see.PRS OBJ apple
"See the apple!"
```

### Adjectives

Adjectives precede nouns:

```text
gorz rosz pingą
big  red  apple
"big red apple"
```

### Adverbs

Adverbs precede verbs:

```text
gial siemąvi
fast run.PRS.1SG
"I quickly run"
```

### Demonstratives

* **dia** = this (proximal)
* **ria** = that (distal)

```text
diapingą i riapingą
PRX.apple COORD DST.apple
"this apple and that apple"
```

### Possession

Add **ų** to a noun or pronoun for genitive:

```text
pingąvių i pingągatųų
apple.1SG.GEN COORD apple.cat.GEN
"my apple and the cat's apple"
```

### Conjunctions

| Clong  | Function |
|--------|----------|
| i      | and      |
| bynię  | banana   |
| lių    | while    |
| zsę    | after    |
| fąsįti | because  |

Examples:

```text
pingą i bynię
apple COORD banana
"an apple and a banana"
```

```text
szanąvi lių siemąti
see.PRS.1SG SIM run.PRS.2SG
"I see while you run"
```

```text
hianąvi zsę siemąti
say.PRS.1SG SEQ run.PRS.2SG
"I talk after you run"
```

```text
niapąvi pingą gor fąsįti si diacziofna
eat.PRS.1SG apple CAUS put.PST.2SG 3SG DST.place.LOC
"I am eating the apple because you put it there"
```

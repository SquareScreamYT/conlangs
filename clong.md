### Phonotactics

* **Syllable structure:** (C)V(C)
* **Onset:** any consonant except /ŋ/ and /ŋʲ/
* **Nucleus:** any vowel
* **Coda:** any non-palatalised consonant

### Orthography

* [j] and [w] are written as ⟨i⟩ and ⟨u⟩ in codas.
* [ʒ] is written ⟨rz⟩ in codas.
* [ç] is written ⟨x⟩ in codas.
* ⟨sz zs/rz cz⟩ may be written as ⟨š ž č⟩.

## Grammar

### Pronouns

|    | 1   | 2   | 3   | NDEF |
|----|-----|-----|-----|------|
| SG | vi  | ti  | si  | ki   |
| PL | vią | tią | nią | ki   |

**Note:** Nouns do not decline for plurality.

### Word Order

Clong is SVO. Objects are marked with **e**, and pronouns stack onto the verb as a suffix. **e** can be dropped before pronouns.

```text
gatų szaną   e   pingą
cat  see.PRS OBJ apple
"the cat sees an apple"
```

```text
szanąvi     e   pingą
see.PRS.1SG OBJ apple
"I see an apple"
```

```text
szanąvi     ti
see.PRS.1SG 2SG
"I see you"
```

### Tense

| Tense   | Suffix |
|---------|--------|
| Past    | į      |
| Present | ą      |
| Future  | ę      |

### Negation

Negation is marked with **ny**:

```text
szanąviny       e   pingą
see.PRS.1SG.NEG OBJ apple
"I do not see an apple"
```

### Questions

Questions are marked with **li**:

```text
liszanąti     e   pingą?
Q.see.PRS.1SG OBJ apple
"Do you see an apple?"
```

### Imperative

Imperatives use **da**:

```text
daszaną     e   pingą!
IMP.see.PRS OBJ apple
"See the apple!"
```

### Adjectives

Adjectives precede nouns:

```text
gorz rosz pingą
big  red  apple
"big red apple"
```

### Adverbs

Adverbs precede verbs:

```text
gial siemąvi
fast run.PRS.1SG
"I quickly run"
```

### Demonstratives

* **dia** = this (proximal)
* **ria** = that (distal)

```text
diapingą  i     riapingą
PRX.apple COORD DST.apple
"this apple and that apple"
```

### Possession

Add **ų** to a noun or pronoun for genitive:

```text
pingąvių      i     pingągatųų
apple.1SG.GEN COORD apple.cat.GEN
"my apple and the cat's apple"
```

### Dative and Instrumental

Add **us** or **un** for dative and instrumental:

```text
gavatįvi     e   bax  liraxsęsusvių
give.PST.1SG OBJ book teach.AGENT.DAT.1SG.GEN
"I gave the book to my teacher"
```

```text
niapąvi     taczun
eat.PRS.2SG spoon.INSTR
"You eat using a spoon"
```

### Conjunctions

| Clong | English |
|-------|---------|
| i     | and     |
| lių   | while   |
| zsę   | after   |
| gor   | because |

Examples:

```text
pingą i     bynię
apple COORD banana
"an apple and a banana"
```

```text
szanąvi     lių siemąti
see.PRS.1SG SIM run.PRS.2SG
"I see while you run"
```

```text
hianąvi     zsę siemąti
say.PRS.1SG SEQ run.PRS.2SG
"I talk after you run"
```

```text
niapąvi     e   pingą gor  fąsįti      si  diacziofnare
eat.PRS.1SG OBJ apple CAUS put.PST.2SG OBJ 3SG DST.place.LOC
"I am eating the apple because you put it there"
```

```text
pe fąsęti      e   reno    kisznanieviųni        sot  ugękųvi
if put.FUT.2SG OBJ uranium water.LOC.INE.1SG.GEN then die.FUT.1SG
"if you put uranium in my water, I will die"
```

### Locative

There are some suffixes that can be used to modify what words are used for.

-na as a suffix is required for any locative. Subcases must be used, including for locative itself.

```text
silunare
table.LOC.LOC
"on the table"
```

```text
cziupnanie
box.LOC.INE
"in the box"
```

```text
iskelnalan
school.LOC.ABL
"from the school"
```

```text
pasarnalas
market.LOC.ALL
"towards the market"
```

### Relative Clauses

Relative clauses are surrounded by **te** and **to**.

```text
gatų te   diagį     e   kisz  to   niapą   e   nyaf
cat  REL- drink.PST OBJ water -REL eat.PRS OBJ meat
"the cat which drank the water is eating the meat"
```

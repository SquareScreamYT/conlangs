[ɨ] can be pronounced as [ə].

### Phonotactics

Vilantic follows a (C)V syllable structure, where:

* C can be any consonant.
* V can be any vowel.

### Allophones

* [n] is pronounced [m] before [p b] and [ŋ] before [k g]
* [Vi] and [Vu] are realised as [Vɪ] and [Vʊ].
* [Vji] and [Vwu] are realised as [V.(j)i] and [V.(w)u].
